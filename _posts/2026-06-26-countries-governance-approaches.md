Some of the big choices that countries make when selecting their approach to governance:
1. _Top-down_ (autocratic) vs _bottom-up in legitimacy_ (democratic).
2. _Capitalist_ vs _communist_ (w/ _socialist_ in the middle) ownership of production.
3. _Individualistic_ (i.e. wellbeing of individual first) vs _collectivist_ (i.e. wellbeing of the whole first).

Examples of governance approaches defined according to the big choices above:
- Liberal Democracy = democratic + capitalist + individualistic
- Social Democracy = democratic + socialist + individualistic/collectivist (balance of both)
- Fascism = autocratic + capitalist + collectivist (though state-directed; debated)
- Communism = autocratic + communist + collectivist
